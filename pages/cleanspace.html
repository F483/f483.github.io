Cleanspace

Cleanspace is an endless runner prototype. You control a little robot that removes space garbarge by bumping it out of orbit.

The game is made for the Game Boy Advance platform. The graphics are incomplete as the artist abandoned the project, the gameplay is however complete.

Gameboy Advance Emulators

  • Windows: VisualBoyAdvance, no$gba 2.7a
  • Mac: Mac Boy Advance
  • Linux: VisualBoyAdvance
  • Android: My Boy! Free
  • iOS: GBA4iOS
